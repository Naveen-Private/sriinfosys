About Us

Who We Are

Established in 2002, Sri Infosys SDN BHD has evolved into a Dynamic Human Resource Consulting Group. The company focuses on the strong process, entrepreneurial vision and has a passion to excel in customer satisfaction. The company is incorporated in APAC region. The company is Headquarted in Kuala Lumpur,with its offshore development and IT resourcing centre in India & Malaysia.

What We Do

We help to serve the clients requirement in a better way with less turn around time. We are here to provide IT Services such as Application Development, Payroll Management, Staffing & Recruitment, Infrastructure Management,Corporate Trainings.We are also expanding to Engineering domain - Electrical and railway projects

Why Choose Us

• Real strategies to increase the amount of contracting resource rather than just looking for a slice of the current market.
• Substantially cheaper costs of services to our clients by removing the premium normally associated.
• Greater control over performance As Remuneration of contractors is directly linked to performance as assessed by our clients.

Our Services

Application Development

We offers full range of application development from defending and working in close collaboration with the customer on the specification to development, testing and writing documentation, including user manuals and help guides.

Staffing Services

With a strong Staff Augmentation team in place.Sri Infosys SDN BHD provides contract staff on a need basis, with the option of converting them to a permanent staff of your organization, after completion of their contract term which could vary from six months to an year or even more.Having hired more than few hundreds of resources, we have expanded horizon in permanent staffing as well.

Outsourced Payroll Services

Sri Infosys SDN BHD Payroll division comprises of professionals dedicated to serving the corporate community, in the area of Payroll Management including administering & managing the salary compensation, leaves, time sheet, all the regulatory compliances.Outsourcing the Payroll process would help the HR department to focus on Strategic Process Management.

Recruitment Process Outsourcing

With our unique RPO Model, clients can outsource end to end recruitment activity to Sri Infosys SDN BHD, be it for the entire organization or any particular business/ project.

Training & Placement Services

As an upcoming system integrator in Malaysia, trainings has always been a core business within Sri Infosys SDN BHD. We help train your staff to be the effective Knowledge Workers of tomorrow’s economy.
